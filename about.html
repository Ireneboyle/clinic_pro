ABOUT US

iMobile clinic is your one stop shop for your eyecare needs. We provide comprehensive eye checkup from the comfort of your homes. We cut off the hassles of waiting long hours in the clinic to see a doctor while helping you keep safe in this pandemic. We ensure our "senior citizens" and those with any form of impairment get the best quality care possible at their convenience. We also organise screening for big organisations helping their staff function at full capacity further preventing low productivity at work and accidents. This to ensure that they work at their best and to prevent accidents during the course of carrying out their job functions daily. Our team of doctors are highly trained and proficient. We ensure all safety protocols are observed during the course of the eye examination. Our professionalism is top-notch and we ensure we leave you satisfied. We also work with various HMO's to ease payment.

Our services include:

  • Refraction
  • Contact lens Clinic
  • Diagnosing and treatment of ocular diseases
  • Sales of quality frames and corrective lenses
  • Sales of digital glasses
